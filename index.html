Dr Damien Przybylski

I love the sun a lot

I am a reseacher at the Max Plank Institute, Gottingen

I work with computers and programs to model the sun and magnetic waves. I'm passionate about modelling things that are happening in and all arount the sun. Sometimes I go on holidays/conferences to colerado to model the sun with other people. I like my job. Its a good job.

Sometimes I like to talk about the sun
